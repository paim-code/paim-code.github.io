Experience

Application Coaching Coordinator

Nonprofit Medical School Mentorship Program

I run a structured coaching program that helps socioeconomically disadvantaged students navigate the medical school application process, from MCAT strategy to school selection to final submissions. The work is as much about data as it is about people.

I analyze applicant cohorts across GPA, MCAT scores, and application quality to identify patterns, flag weaknesses, and build smarter evaluation frameworks. The goal is always to make the process fairer and the outcomes better and to leave behind documentation and protocols that outlast any single cycle.
